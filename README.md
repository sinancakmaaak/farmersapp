# AgriManage - Smart Agriculture Management System 🌾🚜

AgriManage is a comprehensive, data-driven agriculture management platform designed to help farmers and agribusinesses track, optimize, and streamline their daily operations. From soil to spreadsheet, the system provides full control over fields, greenhouses, and financial workflows.

## 🚀 Key Features

*   **Planting & Harvesting Logs (Ekim & Dikim Kaydı):** Track planting schedules, crop varieties, growth stages, and harvest timelines efficiently.
*   **Irrigation Tracking (Sulama Kaydı):** Monitor water usage, irrigation schedules, and soil moisture history to optimize water consumption.
*   **Pesticide & Fertilization Management (İlaçlama & Gübreleme):** Record chemical and organic applications, dates, dosage, and safety periods.
*   **Financial Tracking (Mali Takipler):** Monitor operational costs, revenues, profit margins, and general agricultural expenses.
*   **Inventory Control (Envanter Takibi):** Real-time tracking of seeds, fertilizers, pesticides, fuel, and farming equipment stock levels.
*   **Document & Invoice Management (Dosya & Fatura Takibi):** Store and organize digital receipts, legal documents, agricultural invoices, and certificates.
*   **Field & Greenhouse Monitoring (Tarla & Sera Takibi):** Manage multiple fields and smart greenhouse structures, tracking specific environmental and spatial data.
